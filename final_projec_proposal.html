Game 1: Light

This game is a puzzle based game based on exploration.

Premise

The ISS Shunya has been sent to explore a derelict and ancient ship. Your crew is the prized jewel of the federation. Your sensors have shown that there are no living organisms onboard and it seems the environment inside is habitable. Your captain has asked the away team to take a look. And so here you are.

Mechanics

The game will have a lot of light sources and lots of mirrors, refractors, filters, light sources, etc. that will be used to open doorways, highlight hidden passageways, reveal information.

Game 2: Cubes

In this game you are a 3-D tetris piece that can change shape and fit into absolutely wierd things.

Game 3: Space Dinosaurs

Space dinosuars fight among each other in futuristic settings. Think overwatch.
